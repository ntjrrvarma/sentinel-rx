Trial ID: AZ-2024-ALPHA
Drug: Cardiovax-9 (Hypertension)
Phase: III
Summary: Patient cohort of 500 individuals aged 45-65. 
Efficacy: 88% of patients showed a reduction in systolic blood pressure within 14 days.
Side Effects: 12% reported mild headaches. 2% reported dizziness. Zero critical cardiac events reported.
Data Classification: RESTRICTED - GxP COMPLIANT ENVIRONMENT ONLY.

Trial ID: AZ-2024-BETA
Drug: PulmoBreathe (Asthma)
Phase: II
Summary: Patient cohort of 200 individuals aged 18-40.
Efficacy: 94% reported improved lung capacity (FEV1) after 30 days.
Side Effects: 5% reported dry throat. 1% reported mild insomnia.
Data Classification: RESTRICTED - GxP COMPLIANT ENVIRONMENT ONLY.
